⚡ Phase Relationships

Star vs Delta connection values

Star (Y) Connection

VL = √3 × Vph
IL = Iph

Delta (Δ) Connection

VL = Vph
IL = √3 × Iph
💡 Tip
For the same power, Delta connection draws √3 times more line current than Star connection.
